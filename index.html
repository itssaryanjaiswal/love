💌 Dil Ki Baat 💌

Meri pyaari Rubi,

Mujhe ab samajh aane laga hai ki kuch log zindagi mein achanak nahi aate, woh shayad pehle se humaari duaon mein hote hain… bas milne mein thoda waqt lag jaata hai. Kyunki jis tarah se aap meri zindagi mein aaye ho, usne mujhe pyaar se zyada sukoon par yakeen karna sikha diya hai.

Ab main har cheez pehle jaisa mehsoos nahi karta. Pehle din bas guzar jaate the, ab har din mein kahin na kahin aapka khayaal reh jaata hai. Koi gaana sunu, kahin akela baithu, ya bina wajah raat ko neend na aaye — har jagah dil chupchaap aapki taraf chala jaata hai, jaise use ab aapka raasta yaad ho gaya ho.

Aur sabse ajeeb baat ye hai ki aapne mujhe badla nahi… aapne bas mujhe woh version banne diya, jo shayad main hamesha se tha lekin duniya ke saamne kabhi ho nahi paaya. Aapke saath mujhe kabhi zyada bolne ki zarurat nahi padti, kyunki aap meri khamoshiyaan bhi samajh lete ho.

Kabhi-kabhi sochta hoon ki agar ek din sab kuch bahut mushkil ho jaaye, duniya bahut shor karne lage, log badal jaayein, waqt badal jaaye… tab bhi shayad aapki awaaz woh jagah rahegi jahan mera dil aakar shaant ho jaayega.

Aapse mohabbat karna mere liye kisi film jaisa dramatic ehsaas nahi hai… ye toh jaise dheere-dheere rooh mein utarti hui roshni hai. Bina shor, bina dikhave ke… bas andar sab kuch thoda aur khoobsurat karti hui.

Aur sach kahun, ab mujhe perfect life nahi chahiye. Agar din mushkil bhi ho, lekin raat mein aapse baat ho jaaye na… toh dil ko lagta hai ki sab theek ho jaayega.

— Aapke Saheb Ji 💕
